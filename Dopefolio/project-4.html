Project Overview

Hotels (Fairmont, Andaz) with bustling buffets generate substantial quantities of diverse food items daily but lack a means to estimate demand. Consequently, they often produce excessive unconsumed food, adversely affecting their operations. Our solution furnishes daily food waste statistics and trends to hotel clients, enabling them to make informed decisions to curtail food waste and enhance operational efficiency.

Solution

Given food waste images, classify them to determine their food categories and generate relevant statistics

Clientele

Fairmont, Andaz, etc.

Work summary

  • Project task planning and resources planning to provide timely deliverables.
  • Performed data cleaning, data labeling, and dataset merging to create training/testing datasets.
  • Implemented data pipeline to stream data for training and testing using HDF5 and Python.
  • Trained robust feature extractors using Google SimCLR framework in a Self-Supervised manner.
  • Experimented model training with Triplet loss and Cascaded classifiers to handle low data scenarios.
  • Implemented a One-vs-Rest classifier with Contrastive loss to identify miscellaneous items.
  • Worked with customer success team to verify the model accuracies and the scalability of the solution.
  • Deployed the system on GCP with the help of Docker and integrated a monitoring system using SlackAPI with push notifications.
  • Performed model maintenance to manage model drifts and maintained proper documentations.
