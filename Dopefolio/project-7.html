Project Overview

Identify the most attractive video for a given item and display them as a thumbnail

Solution

Given a candidate video list for an item, rank them based on CR*CTR and recommend top 1 or 2 videos as the item thumbnail on the platform.

Work summary

  • Developed pairwise ranking model using xgboost and evaluated the online model performance using AB testing.
  • Trained Deep interest network to predict CTR of a given item and used it as a deployment and cutoff strategy when deploying videos to items.
  • Through data analysis work, introduced several new methodologies to prune online experiments and methods to filter out low-quality items during deployments.
  • Item level and video level traffic data aggregation and provide insights to improve the overall business product.
