Project Overview

This project is linked to Shopee's Product Video Summarization product line. The main goal is to assess the quality of generated summary videos from lengthy seller-uploaded videos, determining their suitability for online deployment. Additionally, an essential control mechanism is required to maintain video quality throughout the summarization process.

Solution

Create models for predicting image and video quality, which will play a crucial role in evaluating both visual quality and product attractiveness.

These models will serve as rankers and filters within the system, ensuring that the most visually appealing and product-relevant content is prioritized.

Work summary

  1. Designed the quality control system for the PVA product line, including the development and deployment of its associated models.
  2. Developed and experimented video quality evaluation models using TDN, ViT, and FASTVQA.
  3. Developed and experimented image quality evaluation models using koncept512 and hyperIQA.
  4. Researched on utilizing random forest, xgboost, and EBM to make use of image-level features such as brightness, contrast, sharpness, etc. to predict video-level and image-level quality.
  5. Prepared labeling SOPs and datasets for data labeling teams to get proper labels for model training.
  6. Conducted data analysis tasks and case studies to understand data and results.
  7. Conducted rigorous experiments to train models with high-class imbalance datasets.
  8. Resource and timeline planning and setting up KPIs for quarterly model improvements.
  9. Model deployment with Docker and in-house model serving and monitoring tools.
