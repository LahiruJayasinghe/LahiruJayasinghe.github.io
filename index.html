Experience

Shopee Singapore

Senior Algorithm Engineer

Focusing on building and leading AI components for Product Video Summarization product line (PVA) in Shopee platform.

  • Designed several Vision Transformer (ViT)-based solutions for video and image quality prediction, resulting in a 75% reduction in manual labeling costs and a substantial increase in conversion rates.
  • Developed a multi-modality solution for PVA to accurately identify the selling products in videos, achieving a 200qps response rate, directly contributing to a significant 1% boost in orders across deployed pages.
  • Implemented a video ranking solution to identify the user attractiveness towards summarized videos and integrated this as a deployment strategy to PVA resulting in ~5% order uplift across deployed items.
  • Designed a multi-task learning module dedicated to scene classification within videos, and this significantly amplified the PVA product line capability to tailor video generation based on individual user preferences.
  • Data analysis to understand the online performance of deployed items, identifying opportunities for enhanced orders and clicks, driving future model development and ongoing performance improvements.
    • Collaborated closely with stakeholders and external teams, including data annotators, QA engineers, data engineers, and project managers, to facilitate seamless and punctual project deliveries.
